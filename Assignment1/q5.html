The Great Gatsby by F. Scott Fitzgerald is a ***captivating*** novel that delves into the ***decadent*** Jazz Age, exploring themes of love, obsession, and the ***elusive*** American Dream. The protagonist, Nick Carraway, narrates the story of his ***mysterious*** neighbor, Jay Gatsby, whose extravagant lifestyle and ***tragic*** pursuit of Daisy Buchanan embody the novel's critique of the ***corrupt*** pursuit of wealth and status. The book remains an ***enduring*** classic, celebrated for its ***vivid*** prose and ***complex*** characters.
